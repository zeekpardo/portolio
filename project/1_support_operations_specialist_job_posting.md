# Support Operations Specialist

## About Planning Center

Planning Center, established in 2006, has grown to support over 90,000 churches worldwide. As an independent company, we pride ourselves on being debt-free with no outside investors. Churches use our tools to communicate and connect with their congregations, organize information, and coordinate events. We’re committed to building great products that solve real problems using web and mobile products.

Our team enjoys the flexibility of remote work, utilizing tools like Asana, Slack, Notion, and Zoom for collaboration. We gather three times a year at our main office in Carlsbad, CA, fostering our work relationships.

## About the Work

The **Support Operations Specialist** is the hands-on builder and trainer maintaining the infrastructure of the Customer Experience Department. This full-time role splits between technical work (workflow automation, platform configuration, integration projects) and training agents on Front and the CX tool stack. The position reports to the Support Operations Manager.

### Responsibilities

#### Technical Operations

- Build and maintain automated workflows connecting Front, Calendly, Asana, Notion, and Slack using Zapier, n8n, and other API tooling
- Create, test, and implement routing rules, SLA policies, and escalation workflows in Front based on provided specifications or potential workflow improvements
- Support AI chatbot implementation and training

#### Agent Training & Onboarding

- Lead or assist in training for new support agent hires on Front and the rest of the CX tool stack
- Lead or assist in ongoing training to support agents on process changes, product updates, and new tool features
- Maintain and update training documentation based on system changes
- Respond to ongoing agent questions about tools and processes in #support-ops

#### Continuous Improvement

- Monitor workflows for failures and optimization opportunities
- Test new features and tools under guidance
- Propose automation solutions for repetitive manual processes
- Participate in reviews of existing automation to identify improvements

## Qualifications

1. 2+ years working in customer support, preferably at Planning Center
2. Familiarity with Front features like macros and rules
3. Working knowledge in prompting LLMs and AI Agents
4. Familiarity with workflow automation concepts (Zapier, webhooks, integrations)
5. Basic comfort with HTML/CSS
6. Experience with spreadsheets and basic data analysis
7. Demonstrated independent problem-solving skills
8. Excellent written communication and documentation skills

## Competencies

### Core Values

- **Engage with Humility**: Approach new technologies with curiosity and openness to learning and iterating
- **Invest in Our Team**: Build and train agents on solutions that help the CX department do its best work
- **Do Our Best Work**: Build reliable workflows that work correctly and consistently
- **Focus on Customer Impact**: Measure success through improved SLAs, customer sentiment, and agent morale
- **Think Holistically, Work Iteratively**: Understand how systems connect while shipping incremental improvements

### Technical Skills

- **Workflow Automation**: Build workflows, implement basic API integrations and webhook connections, troubleshoot failures, and validate automation accuracy
- **Platform Configuration**: Implement Front configuration changes, test changes thoroughly, and maintain consistent naming conventions
- **Data Analysis**: Pull data from support platforms, create reports and visualizations, and identify patterns in support metrics
- **Technical Documentation**: Collaborate on guides, maintain system documentation, and document troubleshooting steps
- **Problem-Solving**: Work through technical issues with documentation and testing, ask clarifying questions, and escalate when needed
- **Learning Agility**: Learn tools through experimentation, translate documentation into implementation, and grow through iteration and feedback
